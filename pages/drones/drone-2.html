Drone Description

M30T The DJI M301 is an excellent aircraft that is designed to address security concerns and inspection needs. It is smaller in size compared to the DJI Matrice 300 RTK, making it an ideal choice for fast responders who need to act quickly in security concern areas. The M30T is equipped with advanced features such as high zoom capabilities and thermal imaging, which make it highly efficient in conducting inspections and security reconnaissance
