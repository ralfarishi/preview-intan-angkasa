About Us

Welcome to Intan Angkasa Air Service. We have been providing aerial support services across the Indonesian archipelago since 1991 and have become the leaders in this field. Our services include transportation, industrial support, aerial survey, and extreme precision long-line sling loading. With our 30 years of experience and expertise, we guarantee delivering excellent performance and achieving desired outcomes while meeting deadlines.

With a firm commitment to safety, excellence, and environmental responsibility, we stand as a trusted name in the aviation community Our customer-centric approach means we listen attentively to your needs, offering personalized solutions that align perfectly with your requirements.
